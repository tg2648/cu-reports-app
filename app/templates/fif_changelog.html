Faculty Information Form Changelog

2019-20

Both forms:
  • Pre-populated major research fields and current projects from last year's submission (if submitted online).
  • Changed Publications to an open text field.
  • Indicated that in-progress or under review publications may also be included.
  • Added auditing to pre-populated courses.
  • Changed "credits" to "points".
  • Added instructions on how to replace an uploaded CV.
  • Added more example about what can go in the Additional Information section.
  • Made the message displayed after form submission less generic.
Professorial form:
  • Changed "Dissertation title" to "Dissertation topic, area or title".
  • Removed the question about the number of PhD dissertations completed in the last five years.
  • Grants data is provided at the award level.
Lecturer form:
  • Added an optional field to provide information on summer teaching.
PDF copies:
  • Last year's forms can be accesssed online through FIF Archive.
